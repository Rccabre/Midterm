Roberto Cabrera is a Graphic and Web Designer born and raised in Ecuador, currently living in New York. He began his journey as a graphic design student at Bronx Community College in 2011, where he earned an associate's degree in Digital and Graphic Arts in 2013. His creative aptitude led him receive the prestigious J. Kling Award for Excellence in Art. He is currently studying at The City College of New York City, majoring in Digital Design to complete his bachelor's degree. Roberto is fluent in numerous design programs, including Indesign, Illustrator, and PhotoShop, as well as various computer languages, such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript . His trademark is turning complex and difficult ideas into simple, yet beautiful, designs. When not manipulating pixels or coding for web design, you can find Roberto on a soccer pitch or in the gym.
